## Optimal Strategy

### Why Saturday > Sunday
- Sunday = CNY Eve Eve vibes, families prepping
- Saturday morning crowds lighter than afternoon
- If rained out, Sunday backup possible

### Timing
- **Gates open:** 9am
- **Arrive by:** 8:15am (shuttle queues)
- **Flying displays:** Usually 11am-2pm (check schedule)
- **Leave by:** 1-2pm (before heat peak + cranky kids)

### Getting There
- **Shuttle bus** from Changi (free) - recommended
- **Drive** only with group package (parking label required)
- **No Grab/taxi** into venue easily

## Pack List (Kids Edition)

**Essentials:**
- [ ] Ear protection (foam plugs or kids ear muffs) - JETS ARE LOUD
- [ ] Sunscreen + hats
- [ ] Refillable water bottles (water points available)
- [ ] Small snacks (queues for food can be long)
- [ ] Lightweight stroller for 3yo (lots of walking)
- [ ] Wet wipes
- [ ] Portable fan

**Nice to have:**
- [ ] Binoculars (cheap kid ones)
- [ ] Camera with zoom
- [ ] Small umbrella (pop-up showers)
- [ ] Change of clothes for kids

## What Kids Will Love

1. **Static aircraft** - Climb-in opportunities for some planes
2. **Captain Leo & Leonette** - Mascots doing rounds
3. **Interactive zones** - Hands-on activities
4. **Flying displays** - F-35, Apache helicopters, aerobatic teams
5. **Fighter jets up close** - F-16s, Su-30MKM

## Weather Backup

If forecast shows heavy rain for Saturday:
- Option A: Go Sunday morning instead
- Option B: Skip and do Gardens by the Bay CNY display (indoors, runs until March)
