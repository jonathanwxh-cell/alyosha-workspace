# Singapore Airshow 2026 — Family Guide

**⚠️ 3 DAYS AWAY — Feb 7-8 (Sat-Sun)**

## Quick Facts
- **Dates:** Feb 7-8 (public weekend)
- **Location:** Changi Exhibition Centre
- **Tickets:** Adults $39, Kids $19
- **Book:** [SISTIC](https://www.sistic.com.sg/events/airshow0226)

## Why Go With Kids

### Ground Attractions
- 35+ aircraft on static display (commercial, military, drones)
- Walk around, touch, take photos
- Interactive zones with hands-on activities
- Mascots Captain Leo & Leonette doing meet-and-greets

### Flying Displays
- **F-35A Lightning II** (Australia) — stealth fighter debut
- **RSAF Integrated Display** — F-16s + Apache helicopters
- **COMAC C919** — China's new commercial jet
- **Airbus A350-1000** — commercial aviation
- Aerobatic teams from India, Indonesia, China, Malaysia

## Practical Tips

### Getting There
- **Free shuttles** running from designated pickup points
- Driving possible but expect congestion
- Need carpark label from SISTIC if driving
- All passengers need valid tickets

### What to Bring
- Sunscreen (outdoor event, 34°C expected)
- Water bottles
- Hats/caps for kids
- Ear protection for young kids (jets are LOUD)
- Portable fan

### Timing
- Go EARLY — crowds build fast
- **Flying displays: 11am and 3:30pm** (both days)
- Best viewing: find spot 30min before first display
- Leave before 4pm to avoid exit crush

## Best For Different Ages

### Toddlers (3yo)
- Static display area (see planes up close)
- Mascot meet-and-greet
- Interactive family zones
- May need ear protection for flying displays

### Preschoolers (5yo)
- All of the above PLUS
- Flying displays (exciting!)
- Military aircraft (love the fighter jets)
- Cockpit viewing if available

## Don't Miss
1. F-35A flying debut (first time at Singapore Airshow)
2. RSAF combined F-16 + Apache display
3. Static display walk-through
4. Captain Leo photo op
