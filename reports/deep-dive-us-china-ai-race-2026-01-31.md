## The Bottom Line

The "AI race" isn't a single race. It's multiple parallel competitions:

1. **Frontier capability:** US leads, China 12-18 months behind
2. **Compute capacity:** US dominates, but H200 decision could narrow gap
3. **Open-source adoption:** China winning globally
4. **Physical AI deployment:** China has structural advantages

**Investment conclusion:** Don't bet on a single winner. The world is fragmenting into US-led and China-led AI ecosystems that will coexist and compete for decades. Position for both.
