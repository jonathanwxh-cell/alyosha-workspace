## âš ï¸ Common Scams & Pitfalls

### 1. Agent Double-Dipping
- Agent claims to represent you but actually works for landlord
- Charges YOU commission anyway
- **Red flag:** Agent won't clarify who they represent

### 2. Illegal Short-Term Rentals
- HDB min 6 months, Condo min 3 months
- "Weekly" or "monthly" rentals are ILLEGAL
- No legal protection if something goes wrong

### 3. LOI (Letter of Intent) Trap
- Rushed to sign and pay 1-month "good faith deposit"
- If YOU back out, you lose the deposit
- **Always:** Confirm everything before signing

### 4. Unfair Contract Terms
- "All damages tenant's responsibility" â€” reject this
- Take photos/videos on move-in day
- Compare with move-out to avoid deposit disputes

### 5. Unstamped Contracts
- If you don't pay stamp duty, contract is NOT legally enforceable
- You cannot sue or go to Small Claims Tribunal
- **Always pay stamp duty within 14 days**
