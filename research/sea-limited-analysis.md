## Executive Summary

**Current Price:** $113.59 (down 43% from 52-week high of $199.30)
**Market Cap:** $67.2B
**Analyst Consensus:** Strong Buy | Average PT: $182.66-$193.80 (+61% upside)

Sea Limited has completed a remarkable turnaround from -$2B losses (2021-2022) to consistent profitability. All three segments are growing: Shopee (e-commerce), Garena (gaming), SeaMoney (fintech). However, the stock has pulled back 43% from highs, creating a potential entry point.

## Business Segments

| Metric | Q3 2024 | YoY Change |
|--------|---------|------------|
| GMV | ~$27B | +25% |
| Revenue | ~$3.2B | +43% |
| Monthly Active Buyers | +20% | Sustained |

**Market Position:**
- **52% market share** in Southeast Asia (2024, up from 48% in 2023)
- **#1 in Indonesia** (52% of SEA e-commerce)
- **#1 in Vietnam** (71% market share vs TikTok Shop 22%)
- **Expanding in Brazil** (first profitable quarter)

**Management Tone Analysis (Q3 2024 call):**

> "We are seeing high growth across all our three businesses" — Forrest Li

Key language patterns:
- **Confidence indicators:** "very excited," "great position," "strong momentum"
- **Specific metrics cited:** Ad take rate +30bps, ad-paying sellers +10%, revenue per seller +25%
- **Competition acknowledgment:** Mentioned "market rationalization" (code for competitors pulling back)

**Competitive Moat:**
1. **SPX Express logistics** — Half of orders delivered <2 days
2. **Live-streaming e-commerce** — #1 in Indonesia by GMV
3. **YouTube partnership** — Clickable buy buttons in videos (ID, TH, VN)

### 2. Garena (Gaming) — Cash Cow Recovering

| Metric | 2024 | YoY |
|--------|------|-----|
| Bookings (Free Fire) | +30% guidance | Recovery |
| Q3 2025 Bookings | $841M | +51% |

**Analysis:**
Free Fire was in decline post-COVID but has stabilized and returned to growth. This is the cash-generative segment that funds Shopee expansion.

**Management tone:** More subdued than Shopee, but "happy with stabilization" — no longer the drag it was in 2022-2023.

### 3. SeaMoney (Fintech) — High Growth, High Risk

| Metric | Q3 2025 | YoY |
|--------|---------|-----|
| Loan Book | $7.9B | +70% |
| NPL Ratio | Stable | Key watch |
| Revenue | Double-digit growth | |

**Key Insight from Transcripts:**
> "Shopee's large user base in our market makes it highly efficient for us to acquire and serve credit users. Proprietary data from Shopee also allows us to better underwrite risk."

This is the **flywheel**: Shopee data → better credit decisions → more loans → more transactions → more data.

**Risk:** 70% loan book growth is aggressive. NPL "stable" is reassuring, but credit cycles turn.

## Risk Analysis (Talebian Framework)

### Hidden Fragilities

1. **SeaMoney credit risk** — 70% loan book growth in emerging markets
   - NPL "stable" today ≠ stable in downturn
   - If credit cycle turns, this becomes a drag
   
2. **Indonesia concentration** — 52% of SEA GMV from one country
   - Regulatory risk (digital tax, data localization)
   - Rupiah depreciation exposure

3. **Competition resurgence** — TikTok Shop, Lazada (Alibaba), Tokopedia
   - Current "benign competition" may not last
   - Price war could compress margins again

### What Could Go Wrong

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigant |
|------|-------------|--------|----------|
| Credit cycle | Medium | High | Diversified funding, Shopee data |
| Indonesia regulation | Low | High | Multi-market presence |
| Competition returns | Medium | Medium | Logistics moat, ecosystem |
| Gaming decline resumes | Low | Medium | Free Fire stabilized |

## Conclusion

**My Assessment:**

Sea Limited is a **quality turnaround story** at a more reasonable valuation after the 43% pullback. The business fundamentals are strong:
- Market leadership in SEA e-commerce (52% share)
- Proven ability to reach profitability
- Flywheel between Shopee and SeaMoney
- Management executing well (tone analysis confirms confidence)

**Key Watch Items:**
1. SeaMoney NPL ratio (any uptick is a red flag)
2. Shopee EBITDA margins (target: 2-3%)
3. Brazil trajectory (the next growth lever)
4. TikTok Shop competitive response

**Position Sizing Note:**
Given the 70% loan book growth and Indonesia concentration, this is not a "max conviction" position despite the quality. Size accordingly.
