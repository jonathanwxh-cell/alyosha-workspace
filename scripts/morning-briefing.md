# Morning Briefing Template

When triggered, compile and deliver:

## 1. Weather (Singapore)
- Current conditions
- Today's high/low
- Rain forecast
- Air quality if notable

## 2. Markets (if trading day)
- US indices close (S&P 500, Nasdaq, Dow)
- Notable movers in watchlist: NVDA, AMD, AAPL
- Any major overnight news

## 3. Email Digest
- Check Gmail for anything important overnight
- Flag urgent items
- Summarize newsletter highlights if interesting

## 4. Calendar/Reminders
- Any scheduled items for today
- Pending tasks from memory

## 5. Something Interesting
- One cool thing: tech news, science discovery, or local SG event
- Keep it brief and genuinely interesting

## Delivery Style
- Concise, no fluff
- Bullet points
- Actionable items highlighted
- Friendly but efficient tone
